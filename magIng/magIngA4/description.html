Assembly Details

The genome sequence of M. ingens NRRL Y-17630 (CBS 521.90) strain was produced as a hybrid assembly, combining Illumina short reads (approx. 330x coverage) with PacBio RSII long reads (approx. 79x coverage). The assembly consists of 13 nuclear contigs (contig01-contig13; N50=2273.9 kbp) with total size 18.1 Mbp and one contig representing the mitochondrial genome (mtDNA; size=37.7 kbp).

Long reads were assembled by Canu version 1.5 (Koren et al. 2017). The sequencing errors were corrected by two iterations of Pilon (Walker et al. 2014). Further corrections were applied in repeat-rich regions, by replacing appropriate parts of the repeat regions with contigs produced by SPAdes (Bankevich et al. 2012).
