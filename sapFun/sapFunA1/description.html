Assembly Details

The genome sequence of S. fungicola CBS 625.85 strain was produced as a hybrid assembly, combining Illumina short reads (approx. 203x coverage) with MinION (approx. 148x coverage) long-read data. The assembly consists of 5 nuclear contigs (contig01-contig05) likely corresponding to whole chomosomes with total assembly size 20.2 Mbp. One contig represents the mitochondrial genome (mtDNA; size=33.0 kbp).

Long reads were assembled by miniasm (Li 2016) followed by manual curation. The sequencing errors were corrected by Racon (Vaser et al. 2017) and Pilon (Walker et al. 2014).
