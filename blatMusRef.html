Description

This track shows a reference alignment of the mouse genome vs. the human genome constructed from the Mouse Translated Blat Alignments track.

Methods

The track was constructed by selecting the best-scoring blat alignment for each aligned position in the genome. Mouse insertions were included in the reference alignment when they followed the last base of a blat block, although this process has not been entirely perfected.

Credits

This track was produced at UCSC. Mouse sequence data were provided by the Mouse Genome Sequencing Consortium.

References

Kent, W.J. BLAT - the BLAST-like alignment tool. Genome Res. 12(4), 656-664 (2002).
